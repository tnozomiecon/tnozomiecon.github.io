About Me

Hello! I am Nozomi Takeda, a PhD student specializing in quantitative macroeconomics. My research focuses on demographic dynamics, fertility policies, and their macroeconomic impacts.

Research

My research primarily investigates the effects of fertility treatment policies such as egg and embryo freezing on macroeconomic factors, including social security and public debt sustainability. In addition, I am interested in labor markets, firm heterogeneity, and the demographic impacts on macroeconomic policy.

Publications

  • Takeda, N. (2023). "Fertility Policies and Macroeconomic Implications", Economic Review.
